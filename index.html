The web platform for commuter and mobility research.

Welcome to the Itinerum Project

Itinerum is a web platform to allow you to easily make your own spatialized data collection app. It is developed in the TRIP Lab at Concordia University. The Itinerum survey app is available on iOS and Android and is free.

If you would like to test it out, and see your own data, or if you would be interested in using it for your own study, please contact Zachary Patterson. (zachary.patterson@concordia.ca)

Itinerum Platform Workshops

There will be an Itinerum Platform workshop at Concordia University on May 25 and 26. Please email triplab.workshop@gmail.com if you'd like to attend.

Drag & Drop Survey Builder

Easily design a mobile text survey to understand your user demographics. Includes both an entry survey wizard and prompts to ask users about their current itinerary for validating research.

Monitor Live Data Collection

Keep up-to-date with the progress of your survey using our live mapping functionality and tripbreaking. Export the data in .csv for easy use in code or other applications.
